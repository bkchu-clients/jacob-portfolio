Southwest Airlines, Cargo

Transforming a digital experience

Southwest Cargo provides fast and efficient shipping services for valued customers around the country. As a subsidiary of Southwest Airlines, Southwest Cargo focuses on enhancing customer experience with every package that is shipped.

My team consisted of 6 members. Two designers, one project manager and three UI developers. We collaborated with the client’s team to finish this project in 6 design sprints.

The Challenge: How do we redesign Southwest’s Cargo website to keep it competitive with other airlines?

The Solution: We created a responsive web-based experience that emulates Southwest’s digital branding to allow for smooth transitions into next generation designs.

My Role: Along with my Experience Director, Lan Nguyen, I was the experience designer who focused on creating high fidelity wireframes, IA documentation and design patterns.
