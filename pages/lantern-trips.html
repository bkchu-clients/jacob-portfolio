Lantern Trips

Group travel made simple

Lantern Trips is a mobile application that aims to simplify trip planning and become the most trusted source for travel. Although Lantern Trips has conducted market research, defined features, and identified target users, they wish to assess and iterate on what exists in the market so they can deliver a competitive app.

The Challenge: How do we design for collaborative travel during a trip where everyone can share and contribute?

The Solution: We designed a mobile app that allows travelers to create an itinerary, collaborate on activities and share their moments on social media.

My Role: UX Designer, Researcher, Project Manager

How do we make Lantern Trips stand out from their competitors? We began defining areas that could be improved on while integrating Lantern Trips core values:

To cultivate a shared travel community with collaborative trip building.

Create unique travel experiences utilizing trusted recommendations.

Inspire discovery & exploration from travelers like you.

Research

The companies that we thought best represented Lantern Trips values were Bonjournal, TripAdvisor, Tripcast and Trippy. By doing a heuristic evaluation and competitive analysis on current market leaders we realized a few things.

1. Companies that tried to have too many features were not as successful as those who focused on a few core functions.

2. Lantern Trips didn’t have a true direct competitor who tried to solve the hassles of group trip planning.

Interviewing Users -

My team conducted user interviews to empathize with our target audience to find out what aspects of travel inspired them and what pain points they came across. After identifying qualified participants, we ended with a sample pool of 15 people and took their key points and conducted an affinity diagram.

“Being able to share an itinerary and make decisions was a real pain. It was too much information”
